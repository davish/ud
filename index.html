What we do

Uncommissioned Design is a platform that connects people looking for custom furniture with a network of producers who can make it for them.

Our mission

Our mission is to democratize design. We view this as a three-fold endeavour:

  • Make custom furniture more accessible for the average consumer.
  • Lower the barrier-to-entry to the design industry for small-scale producers.
  • Leverage the network effects of a large community to bring down prices for producers and consumers.

How we're doing

To date, Uncommissioned Design has been a passion project. We've been taking and making commissions for our friends (mostly fellow UChicago students for the past year. Our mission to make custom furniture more accessible for everyone has taken us on another journey. We are currently enrolled in the College New Venture Challenge and are actively researching, developing and testing our business model. You'll find a little slideshow of the projects we're the most proud of below.

If you're interested in getting a custom piece made or making a custom piece, please reach out! Before we put together the full-fledged platform we're workshopping the customer-producer interaction and would love to make as many connections and learn as much as possible.
