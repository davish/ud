What we do

Uncommissioned Design is a platform that connects people looking for custom furniture with a network of producers who can make it for them. Our mission is to democratize design by:

  1. Making custom furniture more accessible.
  2. Lowering the barrier-to-entry that currently exists in the design industry for small-producers.
  3. Leveraging the network effects of a large community to lower the prices for everyone--consumer and producer alike.
